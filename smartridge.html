Our all new SmartRidge System consists of all the many features, that you need to help make your life easier and help save and preserve our planet. With sensor weights, cameras, and a LCD 1080p screen size of your choice implmented with the newest version of our NextGen Software. The SmartRidge has it all. The weight sensors implemented on each shelf of your fridge allow our system to calibrate the weight of a product with help from our cameras and figure out how much you may have left of a product and send you a notification informing you of it. The cameras allow the system to read what products you have in your fridge and make a list of them, as well as read a products expiration date and inform you if it is near expiration.

Everything stated is kept track on in the SmartRidge system and can be accessed through the LCD screen, or through our app currently only available for android on the Play Store.
