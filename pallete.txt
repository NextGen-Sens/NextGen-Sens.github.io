Overall Look and Feel of SmartRidge
===================================
The oveerall look and feel should be complex yet simple. The buttons will mainly be 2D with a 3D or animation aspect when you hover over the button.
The website should be loud but also subtle for the user.

Colors
======
For SmartRidge, colors expose personality to our customers and represent that we are energetic and here to help.
These are our color swatches:

- #196275
- #E0E9EB
- #25A7D7
- #76CFEF
- #D676F9
- #153B54

Inconography
============
We will focus on using icons that have a slight hovering flat design with a animational aspect to it, which will compliment the overall look and feel

Typography
==========
We will be using Noto Sans Mono from Google Fonts

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined should chnage color when a user hovers or clicks on one

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be a flat 2D design and will turn into a hovering animational when clicked or hovered on using the chosen outlined colors above
